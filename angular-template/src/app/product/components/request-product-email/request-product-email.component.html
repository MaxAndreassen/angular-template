If you have previously bought assets as a guest on GameAsset.io, you can use this form to request an email which will allow you to re-download any of any these purchased assets.

It is recommended that you login to your account using the temporary password you will have been emailed on your initial guest purchase rather than using this method.

If you have lost your temporary password you can use the forgotten password page to recover your account.

If this email address has any purchase associated with it then an email will have now been sent!
